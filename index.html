Why takaful, not conventional insurance

Takaful replaces interest, uncertainty and speculation with mutual donation (tabarru') and shared responsibility — protection practised as an act of brotherhood.

No Riba — no interest in how the fund is managed or invested.
No Gharar — no hidden terms or excessive uncertainty.
No Maysir — no gambling or speculation with your protection.
